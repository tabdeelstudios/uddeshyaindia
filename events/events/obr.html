uddeshya

One Billion Rising

City : Vellore, Tamil Nadu

‘One in every three women will be beaten or raped during her lifetime’.

Since the past three years, Uddeshya, in its quest to bring about change, has taken up a stance in support of the Global One Billion Rising campaign to end violence against women by organizing a series of events themed along those lines. Masses of students lit candles in solidarity with this global revolution and took an oath - a promise to rebuke the violence against women, a word to trail the path of solidarity and an assurance to bring forth a change.
