Kaagaz

City : Mumbai, Maharashtra

An initiative by Uddeshya Mumbai, Kaagaz is a talk show which aimed at delivering the ultimate experience: a culmination of fresh thoughts and ideas which lead to shattering new perspectives through powerful stories and experiences. With personalities like Dhruv Sehgal and Suhas Joshi captivating the audience, Kaagaz comprised of tales with the eloquence to foster introspection, inspiration, and motivation, which provoke us to look beyond the obvious. A sheet of paper - we believe it is a representation of life itself. The potential to mold into a variety of forms. The ability to go beyond what you are and see what you can become.
