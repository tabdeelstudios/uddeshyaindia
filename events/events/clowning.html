Humanitarian Clowning

Clowning is a fun activity which involved members of Uddeshya dressed up as clowns, engaging underprivileged children in games and other activities while also making them aware of essential issues like health and hygiene. Apart from bringing a smile on those little faces, this serves as a learning experience for our members, educating them about the glaring socio-economic gap between the privileged and underprivileged in our country.
