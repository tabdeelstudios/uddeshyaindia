Genesis

City : Vellore, Tamil Nadu

Awareness is a fundamental factor that influences the actions and decisions taken by people during their youth, thereby inevitably playing a pivotal rule in how their future, as well as that of the country, unravels.

Genesis was a three - day event that systematically tackled Alcoholism, Smoking and Drug Abuse, each day. With a turnout of over 500 people, the event marked its place in history as various dance, music, poetry, and drama performances, addressed the mentioned issues, leaving the audience awestruck with something to think about.
