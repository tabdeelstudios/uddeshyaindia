Zubaan

City : Mumbai, Maharashtra

Nothing can weave magic better than words. Standing by this principle, Uddeshya Mumbai organized Zubaan, a youth-centric open mic event where people were given a platform to voice their deepest fears, insecurities, and opinions. With young poets like Akshata Narayan and Isha Joshi gracing the event, Zubaan drew people from all walks of life and gave them an opportunity to step out of their comfort zones and push their limits.
