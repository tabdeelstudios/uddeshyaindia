High Talks

City : Vellore, Tamil Nadu

As a part of our Campaign Against Substance Abuse(CASA), Uddeshya VIT Vellore collaborated with the Model United Nations Society of VIT University and conducted an event that served as a platform for the youth to voice their perspectives on Substance Abuse through delivering 5-minute speeches in a competition.

With an audience count of over 100 people and 10 competitors, the event turned out to become a massive success, as the participants not only highlighted various social denominators associated with substance abuse but also came up with various solutions and measures to combat the menace.
