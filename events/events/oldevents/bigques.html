The Big Question Uddeshya
A Glance At the Bigger Picture

Discussions lead to debates which lead to solutions. Solutions are what our country and this world needs. Solutions are the need of the hour. Hence the Big Question – a forum for the youth to talk about is-sues and present their views on topics which aren‟t talked about openly – issues like sexual abuse in India, illiteracy , hon-or killing , mercy killing , etc.

The youth needs a voice, this event does exactly that. Questions are raised, arguments are put forward and in the end what remains is an opinionated crowd and plausible solutions. It has gained impetus and popularity on campus and in other chapters in different colleges.

This event has the potential to be one of the biggest open forums to discuss problems that plague us , our society and we feel it will live up to its potential.
