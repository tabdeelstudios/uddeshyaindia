Election Hour #1 Vellore
Regional Parties-Makers or Breakers

The first instalment of the Election hour was conducted on 5th March 2014 by the VIT Uddeshya Chapter as a part of Political Arena 2014,the chief guests for the event were Professor Brijesh Nair and Dr. S SasiKumar who were accompanied by the student representatives Mr. Shivam Negi from F.E.P.S.I, Ms. Stuti from H.L.A and Mr. Abdul Rahman Zafar from Uddeshya. The moderators were Mr. Siddharth Sinha and Mr. Shreyaan Kaushal, and the discussion was focussed on the peopleâ€™s opinion about the role that regional parties play in Indian politics.

Everyone present, including the audience presented their views on the topic. While some thought that the regional parties were a boon, there were others who believed it to be otherwise.

It was finally concluded that although regional parties do play a very essential role in the elections, they are neither the makers nor the complete breakers of the Indian political system.
