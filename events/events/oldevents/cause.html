A Conquest for Awareness

The theme of the event runs along the similar lines as the objectives of our organization i.e. creating awareness about various issues of concern to the youth community. The forte for this event would be to adjudicate the speed analytical skills of the teams.

A quiz, and if you get through, you begin an adventure , a race against other teams . Teams of two in a scrimmage throughout campus!
Seek and ye shall find!

The event is similar to a treasure hunt, where the qualifying teams need to decipher the clues and keys. These clues have a hidden theme which the teams need to identify.

A conquest for greater awareness. Tasks, clues , questions , riddles. Awareness was never this much fun.
