We in Uddeshya create peer educators who spread awareness through our various modules on civic rights and duties, HIV and the stigma surrounding it, substance abuse, sexual abuse etc thus empowering youth or a better tomorrow. The most important aspect of an educator is knowing how to deliver the message. PETP stands for Peer Educator Training Program and in PETP 101 we were taught how to present a module to our peers. Importance of having our own style that we are comfortable with, overcoming nervousness and a few tips on making the session interactive were given by Khushboo Daiyya, Shyam Srinivasan and the Chairman of Uddeshya, Siddharth Sinha.
