AIDS WALK VIT University, Vellore
A Walk Against AIDS

VIT’s NGO, Uddeshya during one of the biggest cultural fest in India, Riviera’14, joined hands against HIV.

It organised an AIDS Walk, where students wearing red started marching from different sides of the campus. A number of students joined them. The walk ended with the start of a street play. This play emphasized the importance of responsible decisions in the face of peer pressure and the unconscious chain of events from escapism to the ultimate abyss, which is HIV-AIDS.

“It’s all about training and making the people capable to solve the problems at a grass-root level. Time as a resource is what we believe in,” says Siddharth Sinha, founder of Uddeshya.
