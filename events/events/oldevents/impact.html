IMPACT '15 Vellore
Inspired People To ACT

IMPACT stands for “Inspiring More People to Act”, an initiative aimed at empowering youth by actively involving them in the decision making processes of our country. The youth of today are strong and willful, and are the first straw drawn in the path towards rightful change.

Each event organized in IMPACT had a different approach to youth empowerment. With various sponsors including Schneider Electric and Oikos International, the event received the sufficient support to entertain participants numbering 450. With a workforce of 120 members, the organizing team functioned harmoniously in all departments to pull off the event at a grand scale. The Guest Speaker Panel included dignitaries such as Mr. Prakash Rohera, Mr. Ritesh Sharma and Mr. Sriram Ayer, all of whom profoundly commended the initiative by Uddeshya, the organizer. IMPACT does not simply demonstrate the tenacity of young adults but teaches them and motivates them to make a difference.
