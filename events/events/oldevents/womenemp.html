Big Ques- Women Empowerment Uddeshya
Women Empowerment

WOMEN EMPOWERMENT has been a topic in debate from a very long time and will continue being so in the future as well till every single person realizes its importance. The women of the country should also clarify that they want equality and not preference. Keeping the issue of empowering women in the society in mind Uddeshya organized a big question where in participants of VIT University expressed their views. The problems faced by women of different sectors were raised and more importantly solutions of the same were discussed. The most heated argument was the ‘Misuse of maternity leave’. Some of the conclusions of the discussion were that proper patrolling by policemen can help reduce the no. of crimes on women, making a domestically abused woman financially independent etc.
