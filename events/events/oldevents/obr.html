One Billion Rising VIT University, Vellore
What is OBR?

So it is roughly considered that the population of the world is 7 Billion, among which 3 billion are women.Through past researches and statistics,it is found that 1 out of every three women is sexually harassed.We the members of Uddeshya perform this event to give a tribute to those who have suffered this harassment and not accepted by the society. We showed a documentary video followed by a skit play.In the documentary video we showed some minimal posters, short videos related to the topic and a survey which we conducted. In the skit we highlighted topic and showed how things actually take place in our society and how social stigma, money and power can be a barrier to truth and also realized how important it is to raise our voices .Our main motto is to educate people and come forward to stop such ruthless activities .And at the end we lit up balloons in hope to help make a better future.
