Beyond The High

City : Vellore, Tamil Nadu

Nothing appeals more than laughter and good humor to the youth of today. This event was a package of comedy, sarcasm, and rhetoric, that intrigued the audience and kept them on their feet, while subtly highlighting the social undercurrents associated with substance abuse, as well as the dangers it poses, and the effect that it has on the loved ones of people who indulge in the same.

With a turn up of over 80 participants, 3 comedians and 2 poets, the participants resonated with positive energy as they shared their reviews and opinions.
